General Problem

The library at STI College Alabang currently relies on traditional, manual methods for managing its collection and student transactions. This dependency on physical logging leads to several operational inefficiencies that affect both staff and students.

Specific Problem
 
 1. Operational Inefficiency – The current manual physical logging of library transactions at STI College Alabang is time-consuming and prone to delays, affecting both students and library personnel.
 
 2. Data Inaccuracy – Dependency on traditional record-keeping leads to potential human errors in inventory tracking, which results in outdated book availability data and mismanagement of library resources.
 
 3. Accessibility Gaps – Students lack a centralized online portal to verify real-time book availability or view their borrowing status without physically visiting the library, limiting the convenience of library services.


Objectives

General Objectives

To design and develop Lumina, a web-based library book reservation, borrowing, and inventory management system with QR-based scanning for STI College Alabang. The system aims to enhance efficiency in library transactions through a centralized online platform, improve inventory tracking accuracy, and provide seamless real-time access to library services for both students and librarians.


Specific Objectives
Admin

To integrate a secure login module using Microsoft O365 authentication for identity verification.

This objective ensures a secure and standardized sign-in process by leveraging institutional accounts, verifying that only authorized personnel can access administrative tools.

To develop a Centralized Dashboard that provides a real-time overview of the library’s total book inventory and current stock status.

This objective provides administrators with an immediate snapshot of library resources, enabling efficient stock monitoring and data-driven inventory management.

To implement a Circulation Desk that utilizes QR-Code scanning for both student identification and book processing.

This feature automates the link between the borrower and the book, significantly reducing manual data entry and accelerating transaction times at the counter.

To develop a Borrow History module that maintains a comprehensive digital record of all active, returned, and overdue books.

This objective aims to maintain a transparent digital ledger that allows administrators to monitor borrowing trends and effectively manage outstanding book returns.

To establish a User Directory for managing the profiles and system access levels of all registered students.

This objective provides a secure database for student management, ensuring that library services are accessed only by verified members of the STI community.

To develop a Settings and Policies module for the customization of borrowing limits, reservation rules, and academic categories.

This allows administrators to configure system rules and institutional policies dynamically to align with the library’s specific operational requirements.

To develop an Audit Log that captures a detailed history of all administrative actions performed within the system.

This provides a transparent audit trail of all system modifications, helping to prevent unauthorized changes and ensuring accountability across administrative operations.


Student

To integrate a secure login module using Microsoft O365 authentication for identity verification.

This objective provides a secure sign-in process by leveraging institutional accounts, ensuring that only verified students can access their personal library accounts.

To provide a Personalized Student Dashboard for viewing digital library cards and tracking personal borrowing activities.

This objective provides students with a dedicated portal to manage their library profile, view their digital ID, and track their current borrowing status in real-time.

To develop a Searchable Catalog that displays the real-time availability of all books in the library collection.

This objective improves accessibility by providing an online catalog where students can browse and reserve books remotely before visiting the library.

To maintain a Personal Borrowing History that identifies active, returned, and overdue books.

This objective helps students stay organized by providing clear visibility of their transaction history and upcoming return deadlines.
